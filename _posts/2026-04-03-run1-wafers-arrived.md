The wafers are here.

After 12 weeks at GlobalFoundries, the Run 1 wafers have been manufactured and returned to us. This is the moment where your designs stop being GDSII files and start being real silicon.

<!-- Image not found: receiving-wafers-tim-foup -->

## What Happens Next

The wafers now go through dicing, sorting, and inspection. Each wafer gets cut into individual dies, and those dies get sorted and verified before being packed for shipping. We expect to start shipping bare dies in a few weeks, with chip-on-board packaged parts following shortly after.

## Run 2 is Open

If you missed Run 1 or want another slot, [Run 2 is now open on Crowd Supply](https://www.crowdsupply.com/wafer-space/gf180mcu-run-2). Early bird pricing is available through **30 April 2026**:

* **1x1 (full) slot**: $7,000 ($7/die)
* **0.5x1 (half width) slot**: $4,000 ($4/die)
* **1x0.5 (half height) slot**: $4,000 ($4/die)

Every slot includes 1,000 dies. Half slots are new for Run 2.
