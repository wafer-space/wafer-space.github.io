The Run 1 wafers have been diced and we are now sorting individual dies. Here is a look at the process.

<!-- Image not found: sorting-bare-dies-robot -->

## How Die Sorting Works

After the wafers are cut into individual dies, each die needs to be picked up, inspected, and placed into tape-and-reel for shipping. This is handled by an automated sorting robot that works through the diced wafer one die at a time.

<!-- Image not found: sorting-bare-dies-progress -->

## Shipping Timeline

We expect to start shipping bare dies soon. Some of you may already have received shipping notifications from Crowd Supply. Chip-on-board packaged parts will follow once the wire-bonding process is complete.
