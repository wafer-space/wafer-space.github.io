Run 1 bare dies have shipped, and now we are working through the chip-on-board (COB) packaging for customers who ordered that add-on. Here is a look at the process.

<!-- Image not found: chip-on-board-wirebonding -->

## The COB Process

Each die is placed onto a small PCB and wire-bonded to connect the die pads to the board traces. Once bonded, the die and wires are protected with an epoxy glob-top. The result is a packaged part you can plug into your own board and start testing immediately, no specialized equipment needed.
