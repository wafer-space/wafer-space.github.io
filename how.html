The Manufacturing Journey

1. Submit Your Design

Upload your GDS files (Graphic Design System) to wafer.space. These files contain the complete layout of your integrated circuit design.

2. Mask Preparation

wafer.space processes your design and creates optimized mask GDS files. These are sent to GlobalFoundries for photomask creation.

3. Wafer Fabrication

GlobalFoundries creates photomasks and fabricates silicon wafers using their advanced 180nm GF180MCU process in Singapore.

4. Die Cutting & Sorting
